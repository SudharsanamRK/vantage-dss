# VANTAGE
VANTAGE is a high-performance, operational OS designed for modern aquaculture management.

# Output Modules:
**1. Dashboard**

 > Serves as the central mission control, providing a high-level aggregation of real-time telemetry, critical alerts, and key performance indicators (KPIs) to assess overall farm health at a glance.

**2. Pond Mapping**

 > Offers a spatial visualization of the farm's physical layout, allowing operators to track environmental variables and stock status geographically across different pond units.
